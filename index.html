Non-Operational Vehicle Registration

Operational vehicles are not the only automobiles that require state registration. Residents in Arizona also need to present paperwork to the DMV for non-operational cars.

Non-operational vehicles are those that will not be hauled, driven, parked, or stored on public roadways for the duration of their registration year. Temporary Registration

Residents of Arizona who lack the necessary paperwork to register their vehicle may apply for a 90-day interim registration.

While they gather the necessary paperwork to get a permanent vehicle registration certificate, residents of Arizona have 90 days to lawfully drive their car on state highways and roads using a temporary registration certificate.
